Discoveries & Insights

Summary

Overall, we can make some conclusions regarding the residential sales in the city of Boston in the last two decades. The vast majority of the transactions are condos in the inner city zip codes. These buildings have an average annual prices of about a million dollars. These findings confirm the arguments made in the Boston Globe article that shows how building housing for the rich in Boston keeps pushing low income citizens into the outskirts of the city where there are not many housing options.
